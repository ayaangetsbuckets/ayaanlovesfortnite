Why Basketball?

Basketball is more than just a game for me. It’s a way to stay active, build friendships, and push myself to improve. The sport has taught me teamwork, discipline, and perseverance — skills that also help in school and life.

Tips to Get Better

Here are some key areas that can make a big difference in your game:

  • Dribbling: Practice with both hands. Start with basic ball-handling drills like crossovers, figure 8s, and two-ball dribbling.
  • Shooting: Focus on form first. Use the "BEEF" method (Balance, Eyes, Elbow, Follow-through).
  • Conditioning: Run sprints, work on footwork ladders, and do endurance training to keep up your stamina on the court.
  • Defense: Stay low, move your feet quickly, and always watch your opponent’s waist instead of the ball.
